Aerovisualizer v0.0.143 beta

 

Aerovisualizer was built for students and teachers of aerospace engineering. Its purpose is to reinforce concepts by presenting them in interesting and engaging ways. 3D animations are displayed to complement the dry equations found in textbooks and online.

Currently, Aerovisualizer requires a browser and an internet connection. Add it to your mobile device's home screen for easy use. The source code is free to download and copy at github.com/eastmanrj/aerovisualizer. If you find Aerovisualizer useful, please give the project a star!

Send bug reports or suggestions to eastmanrj@users.noreply.github.com. Please include "Aerovisualizer" in the subject.
