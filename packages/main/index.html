Aerovisualizer

v0.1.2 beta

Aerovisualizer is made for students and teachers of aerospace engineering. Its purpose is to reinforce concepts by presenting them in interesting and engaging ways. It is absolutely FREE!

Aerovisualizer requires a browser and an internet connection. Add it to your mobile device's home screen for easy use. Download the free source code at github.com/eastmanrj/aerovisualizer. If you like Aerovisualizer, please give it a star!

Send bug reports or suggestions to eastmanrj@users.noreply.github.com. Please include "Aerovisualizer" in the subject.
